About What I Do and Who I Am

Currently, I am a General Manager at Auto Parts Outlet, a store that specializes in selling automotive body parts to local Businesses and consumers I am In charge of the day to day operations and sales of the store. My other responsibilities include implementing online marketing campaigns for all 16 store locations, Managing all social media accounts, and Generating new promotions

My Sales & Marketing skills are simply a small part of what I can offer, I am an accomplished front-end developer with nearly 4 years of experience using HTML5, CSS3, SASS, Bootstrap, jQuery and building successful single & multi-page applications with modern technologies like React, Redux, Node JS, etc. Proven ability in building smooth User Interfaces and creating RESTful APIs that can deliver JSON or XML content.Proficient in a wide range of Building tools,Testing tools, and Version control tools. I am a continious learner that keeps up with new technologies and changes to languages and frameworks.

During my free time, I enjoy spending time with my 2-year-old son going to the museum and park, I love to watch online videos to improve my own skills with my guitar, photoshop or automotive projects. I enjoy reading books a lot as well either regarding sales or time management. If I find myself with time during the weekend I enjoy taking photos I love finding new locations to have photoshoots with new models.
